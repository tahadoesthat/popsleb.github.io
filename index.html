About

POPs LEB is a part of the global leading provider company of Point of Sale solutions, POSBANK.

POSBANK is dedicated to being a globally trusted leader in the in-store solution industry, aiming to satisfy customers with best-in-class products and services.

They provide innovative POS systems and custom solutions, focusing on customer-focused engineering to help businesses succeed.

POSBANK has over 20 years of proven quality and design experience in POS solutions.
