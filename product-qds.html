POPS QDS (Queue Display System)

Transform waiting time into sales. An easy-to-install system directly linked to POPS Restaurant to display order status and promotional videos.

Why Choose POPS QDS?

Keep your customers informed and entertained while they wait.

Direct Sync

Linked directly with the POPS Restaurant system. Updates happen automatically as orders progress.

Promotional Videos

Play up to 3 videos simultaneously alongside order info. Convert waiting time into a sales opportunity.

Multi-Language

Fully supports both Arabic and English languages for diverse customer bases.

Status Updates

Clear "Now Serving" and "In Process" lists help reduce customer anxiety and crowding at the counter.

Grab Attention

Visual notifications ensure customers pick up their orders promptly, keeping the service area clear.

KDS Integration

Works in perfect harmony with the Kitchen Display System (KDS) for a complete workflow loop.

Seamless Customer Flow

Create a professional environment where your customers feel valued and informed.

Step 1: Order Registration

Customer places order via POPS Restaurant System.

Step 2: "In Process"

Order number appears in the "In Process" list on the big screen.

Step 3: "Now Serving"

When the kitchen marks it done, the number flashes in "Now Serving" with an alert sound.
